🔓 Interview Activity – Unlocked!

Interview your partner using these questions and ask follow-up questions:

1. Have you read any good books recently?
→ What was it about? Would you recommend it?
2. Have you already chosen your next book?
→ What made you choose it? Where did you hear about it?
3. Have you just finished reading a book?
→ How did it end? Were you satisfied with the ending?
4. Did you ever start a book years ago and still haven't finished it?
→ Why did you stop? Do you think you'll ever finish it?
5. Have you ever read the ending of a book before finishing it?
→ Why did you do that? Did it ruin the book for you?
6. Have you bought a book and never read it?
→ Why haven't you read it? Do you still plan to?
7. Have you finished the book you're reading yet?
→ How much is left? Are you enjoying it?
